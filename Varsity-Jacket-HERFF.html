HOLLOWAY HERITAGE

wool fabric

Made from the finest 24 oz. Melton wool blend with a fiber content of 80% virgin wool and 20% nylon, which improves performance and reduces pilling.

LINING/INTERIOR ZIPPER ACCESS

An improved quilted lining is used to maintain warmth & comfort. Access zipper for easy decoration.

leather/vinyl

The finest top grain steerhide leather specially treated to resist scuffs and cracks. The team carefully cuts around natural flaws and imperfections on the skin to provide the best looking leather around.

CONSTRUCTION

Double needle felled shoulder and sleeve seams resulting in a stronger and more durable seam than a single needle stitched seam can provide.

pockets

Matching quality, real leather is used for the double besom welt pockets. Offset with wide wool facing inside the pocket.

comfort

Ample sizing for comfortable fit.

knit trim

Our knit trim elasticity memory has top quality resistance to pilling.

ADDITIONAL CUSTOM OPTIONS & DETAILING:

LENGTHS

  • Standard sizes available for Adults XS–6X and Youth S–XL
  • Various length adjustments available for body and sleeves starting at 2”
  • 2” longer body and sleeve is standard tall
  • 2” shorter body and sleeve is standard short

CORDAGE AND BRAIDS

  • Cordage is available around shoulder inserts and pocket trim
  • Braid is available down sides of shallow and deep scallop hoods, all around sailor collar and straight-across zippered hood

HOODS AND COLLARS

  • Detachable snap-off hood available with stand-up knit collar
  • All leather or vinyl Byron collars are available
  • Wool Byron collar with leather or vinyl underside is available

LINING AND INSIDE POCKETS

  • Nylon or quilted lining is available; black is standard
  • Inside patch pocket, slash single-welt and zippered double-besom pockets are available
  • Hidden embroidery access zipper in lining
  • Reversible construction to oxford nylon is available; some colors not available
  • Brass zipper front closure
