FAQS

Can I use my Pacific Headwear account login?

To create a smooth ordering experience, you need an Augusta Sportswear Brands account. Your Pacific Headwear account login will not work.


If you have received a new login from customer service, please try logging in at the top to verify your account works.


Don't have a login? Sign up now!

How do I open a new account?

If you had an account with both Augusta Sportswear and Pacific Headwear, your account number going forward will be your Augusta account number.


If you did not have an account with Pacific Headwear, but had one with Augusta Sportswear, you can now use your Augusta account number to order Pacific Headwear items.
