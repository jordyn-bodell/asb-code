The high-quality, moisture wicking t-shirt that, thanks to true hue technology™, is guaranteed to hold up to screen printing without color bleed or your money back. Our products with true hue technology™ are designed to prevent dye migration making decorating more efficient with color reliability you can count on, guaranteed!
We will replace any true hue technology™ style that has color migration post decoration if decorated under normal operating conditions*.

*It is always best to screen print polyester fabric at the lowest required flash and curing temperatures. Low temperature curing and migration resistant inks will achieve the best results. Remember to always follow the ink manufacturer’s directions for curing time and temperature. Generally, use inks which are cured at temperatures below 320°F, which also minimizes fabric shrinkage.
