Up your game with our uniform decoration.

To decorate, you must place a minimum order of 12 uniforms tops for Premium Transfers and 24 for Screen Print

Decoration Guidelines:
— We offer 4", 6", 8" and 10" numbers in select fonts.
— Embroidery below neck or on short varies based on stitch count and quantity.
— Base price of $5.00 up to 5,000 stitches
— Decoration details and prices excludes Russell Athletic Football

Reorders:
— Minimum 6 per style at current pricing at the time of re-order.
— 1–2 pieces incur a 50% upcharge on the decoration.
— 3–5 pieces incur a 25% upcharge on the decoration.
— Please provide previous P.O. number or previous order number.

Hemming:
— Style 33347M Russell Delux Relaxed Fit Pant, is available to be hemmed by request.
— There is no charge for this service.
— P.O. must state the pant is to be hemmed and:
— Note the requested hemmed inseam length to the nearest inch
— State whether the pant is to have an Open Bottom Hem or an Elastic Bottom Hem
— Please email P.O. to sales@augustasportswear.com
