Premium Stock Layouts

Want a great looking cap but aren't sure of a design? We make it easy:

Premium Stock Layouts are designed for embroidery but are available in any of our decoration styles (Patches, FlexStyle®, etc.). Pricing and turnaround time varies based on decoration style.

 

1) Choose the layout that best represents your brand or adventure 

2) Click layout image for example 

3) [Optional] Replace design element (highlighted pink) with a Premium Stock Icon of your choice. 

4) Personalize with your own Text and Thread Colors or PMS Colors 

5) Send us your PREM Number, ICON Number(s), Wording and Colors
