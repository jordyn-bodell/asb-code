WHAT IS true hue technology®?

Our products with true hue technology® are designed to help prevent dye migration so you can decorate confidently. It’s color reliability you can count on, guaranteed!

true hue technology® is based on the use of Cationic Dyeable Polyester (CDP) rather than common Disperse Dyeable polyester. CDP is manufactured by a specific process which adds anionic (negatively charged) groups during polymerization of the CDP fiber. They say “Opposites attract”, and that’s definitely the case with anionic (negatively charged) groups in our true hue technology® fibers when they’re dyed using cationic (positively charged) dyes.

Cationic Dyed polyester has improved color fastness properties compared to Disperse Dyed polyester due to this positive and negative attraction.

BENEFITS:

  • Prevent dye migration
  • Decorate with confidence
  • Bright decorations stay bright
  • Excellent color retention over time
