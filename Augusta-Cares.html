A history of caring

Founded in 1974 in Grovetown, Georgia, Augusta Sportswear Brands has a long history of giving back to communities around the country. From disadvantaged schools and organizations to the Special Olympics, Boys and Girls Club and the YMCA, we’re committed to helping others and supporting those in need.

goods for good

As a leading multi-brand sport uniform provider, as well as off-field fanwear, bags and lifestyle apparel, we have the unique ability to provide teams uniforms, or everyday apparel such as fleece, tee’s and outerwear for selected organizations seeking assistance.

how it works

Simply fill out the nomination form below and a representative be in touch with next steps. The selection process can take up to 30 days after submission so please be patient.
