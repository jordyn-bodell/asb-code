Q: Why did Russell Athletic and Augusta Sportswear partner together?

A: Russell has an outstanding track record for high quality uniforms and has built a sterling reputation as a top supplier in the team uniform space. Augusta Sportswear will put its industry leading service model behind the Russell brand to deliver an unmatched offering to the industry.

Q: What Russell product categories and uniforms will be offered?

A: Augusta Sportswear Brands offers Russell Athletic product in stock for basketball, baseball, softball and basics categories. In addition, sublimation athletic uniforms are also offered for the above mentioned categories that can be customized for your team. In early 2019, we will be offering uniforms for the football category.

Q: Can I order Russell items on the same order as Augusta, Holloway, and High Five product?

A: Yes, you can order all brands together. Russell Athletic product is eligible for the Augusta Sportswear freight programs including Fair & Simple with free freight starting at $200. Russell Athletic items will be on a separate shipment and invoice.
